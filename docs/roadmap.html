Product direction

Roadmap

The public RICE roadmap is complete through Wave 4. New ideas are separated from the active roadmap until they have a clear local-first product case.

The next active track is v1.1 Pokédex-first: a completion-focused home, richer Pokémon fiches, then carefully sourced Pokédex metadata.

Wave 1 - Polish

Empty states, regional chips, multi-level progress and fuzzy keyboard search.

Wave 2 - Activation

Onboarding, enriched Pokedex status and narrative filters.

Wave 3 - Card layer

Card model, auto caught promotion, Pokemon drawer and full Pokemon page.

Wave 4 - Delights

Artwork switcher, badges, profiles, pocket print and regional themes.

Post-1.0 work

Engagement features already on main.

Focus sessions

Short guided completion loops stored in localStorage.

Next best action

Transparent recommendations shared by Focus and Statistics.

Hunt list

Persisted search targets with high-priority ranking.

Not active

Known but postponed.

Social sharing, public profiles, market prices, daily streaks and scraper-heavy Pokedex descriptions are intentionally outside the active roadmap.
