Product direction

Roadmap

The public RICE roadmap is complete through Wave 4. New ideas are separated from the active roadmap until they have a clear local-first product case.

The v1.1 Pokédex-first home and richer Pokémon fiches are now shipped. The next active track is carefully sourced Pokédex metadata.

Wave 1 - Polish

Empty states, regional chips, multi-level progress and fuzzy keyboard search.

Wave 2 - Activation

Onboarding, enriched Pokedex status and narrative filters.

Wave 3 - Card layer

Card model, auto caught promotion, Pokemon drawer and full Pokemon page.

Wave 4 - Delights

Artwork switcher, badges, profiles, pocket print and regional themes.

v1.1.0

Pokédex-first workflows now shipped.

Completion home

Dashboard, next actions and quick filters keep Pokédex progress visible first.

Richer fiches

Drawer and full page share status controls, linked forms, notes and collapsible cards.

Mobile fiche

Status and forms stay reachable before long card sections on narrow screens.

Next local-first track

Dresseurs stay optional.

The next direction adds manual Trainer Card exchange: a local tab, explicit file import/export and identity-based updates without accounts, public profiles or automatic sync.

v0.1 local card

Create, edit and export your own Trainer Card from the Dresseurs tab.

v0.2 received contacts

Import cards from other collectors and update by stable trainer identity.

v0.3 trade context

Compare wants and trade lists locally, without changing collection state.

Not active

Known but postponed.

Social sharing, public profiles, market prices, daily streaks and scraper-heavy Pokedex descriptions are intentionally outside the active roadmap.
