Product surface

Features

Pokevault is built around the Pokedex, then layers physical cards, binders and progress workflows on top.

Collection grid

Browse the National Pokedex with region chips, narrative tags, fuzzy search and keyboard shortcuts.

Pokédex status

Use compact Cherche, J'ai and Double actions while keeping backward-compatible progress JSON.

Pokemon drawer

Open a side panel for identity, trade actions, linked forms, personal notes, owned cards and focus priority.

Full Pokemon page

View artwork, names, Cherche/J'ai/Double actions, alternate forms, personal notes, type matchups and collapsible cards for one entry.

Physical card catalog

Attach set, number, variant, language, condition, quantity, notes and image URLs to a Pokemon.

Binder planner

Create local binders with a 3×3 · 10 feuillets default, optional custom grids and automatic regional splits such as Kanto 1 and Kanto 2 when a binder is too small.

Focus sessions

Generate a short six-target session from missing entries and jump directly to each target.

Hunt list

Mark active searches, set high priority and keep a short note per Pokemon.

Trainer Cards

Create a local card, export it as a file, import received Dresseurs and keep a searchable local contact book with Double, Vu chez and Match context, without accounts or server sync.

Product tour

Start with a compact five-step tour that explains Cherche, J'ai, Double, Dresseurs, manual imports and local files before asking for region and tracking density.

Badges and stats

Track global, regional and badge progress with transparent next-action recommendations.

Profiles

Maintain separate local Pokedex profiles for main, casual, shiny-only or other workflows.

Print view

Generate printable binder, regional or pocket A5 checklists.

Backup and restore

Export or import the full local collection state as versioned JSON.

Pokedex-first stance

Cards enrich Pokemon entries; they do not replace them.

Version 1.1.0 keeps completion workflows first: dashboard, recommendations, filters and Pokemon fiches all prioritize Pokedex state before card inventory. Marketplace pricing, public wishlists and hosted accounts remain outside the active scope.
