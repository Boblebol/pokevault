Product surface

Features

Pokevault is built around the Pokedex, then layers physical cards, binders and progress workflows on top.

Collection grid

Browse the National Pokedex with region chips, narrative tags, fuzzy search and keyboard shortcuts.

Pokédex status

Use compact Cherche, Capturé and Double actions while keeping backward-compatible progress JSON.

Pokemon drawer

Open a side panel for identity, trade actions, linked forms, personal notes, owned cards and personal hunt priority.

Full Pokemon page

View artwork, names, Cherche/Capturé/Double actions, alternate forms, personal notes, type matchups and collapsible cards for one entry.

Physical card catalog

Attach set, number, variant, language, condition, quantity, notes and image URLs to a Pokemon.

Binder planner

Create local binders with a 3×3 · 10 feuillets default, custom grids, regional splits such as Kanto 1 and Kanto 2, and optional Familles layouts that keep evolution stages aligned with intentional empty slots.

Badge missions

Follow a team badge from the gallery, surface the required Pokemon in Collection and jump directly to missing targets.

Hunt list

Mark active searches, set high priority and keep a short note per Pokemon.

Trainer Cards

Create a local card with optional Instagram, Facebook or phone contact links and automatically shared unlocked badges, export it as a file, import received Dresseurs and keep a searchable local contact book with Double, Vu chez and Match context, without accounts or server sync.

Product tour

Start with a compact five-step tour that explains Cherche, Capturé, Double, Dresseurs, manual imports and local files before asking for region and tracking density.

Badges and stats

Track global, regional and badge progress with transparent next-action recommendations, including Souvenirs de Kanto badges for Rouge/Bleu champions d'arene, Conseil 4 and rival teams, plus Or/Argent badges for Johto, Kanto post-game, Peter and Silver. Badge Side Quest V1 adds a badge gallery with sealed badges until unlock, Pokemon requirement previews, active badge missions in Collection and current gallery labels on each Trainer Card while keeping shared badge payloads compact. The base-version nostalgia catalog continues sans remakes with Rubis/Saphir, Diamant/Perle, Noir/Blanc, Noir 2/Blanc 2, X/Y, Soleil/Lune, Epee/Bouclier and Ecarlate/Violet.

Profiles

Maintain separate local Pokedex profiles for main, casual, shiny-only or other workflows.

Print view

Generate printable binder, regional or pocket A5 checklists.

Backup and restore

Export or import the full local collection state as versioned JSON.

Pokedex-first stance

Cards enrich Pokemon entries; they do not replace them.

Version 1.3.0 keeps completion workflows first: dashboard, recommendations, filters and Pokemon fiches all prioritize Pokedex state before card inventory. Marketplace pricing, public wishlists and hosted accounts remain outside the active scope.
